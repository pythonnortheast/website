Python North East

Introduction

Welcome to the site of the North East of England's Python community. We're a group who promote the use of the Python programming language. We organise monthly meet-ups and other community events and aim to be a focal part of the wider Python community in the North East. We're also super friendly - beginners and non-Pythonistas are especially welcome to our events and to participate in our discussions.

Our next meet up: Meet-ups PyNE Apr 2015 - ORM magic: SQLAlchemy

Wed 8th Apr 2015 at Campus North

This month it's Alex Kavanagh @ajkavanagh to tell us about SQLAlchemy.

What's so great about SQLAlchemy? Come and find out why this slightly boring, but amazingly useful, piece of technology will help you connect to (almost) any SQL database, write queries like the pros in an (almost) database agnostic fashion, and supercharge your development with (almost) all web frameworks* in Python.

Alex will briefly cover:

  • What going on
  • How to connect and model your database
  • How queries work, particularly if you already understand SQL
  • Handy features like database reflection.

*(Django likes to be different).
